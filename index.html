Our mission:

MakeFoodNotWar believes that access to healthy, affordable food is not a privilege; it is the dignified right of all in our community. Our mission is to enable better access to healthy food options by supporting existing programs that provide meals and resources to children, families and the disenfranchised members of our community.

Food is our Passion. That passion led them to develop the Luv My Avo line of avocado oil products with the corporate mandate of committing a percentage of revenue towards MakeFoodNotWar, believing that for-profit companies have a responsibility to positively affect the world around us.

As we build our vision and network of support, we hope you will join us on this journey!
